## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of Traffix Node seriously. If you have discovered a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

**Do not file a public issue.**

Please report any security issues directly to our security team via email at **security@traffix.uz** (or your contact email).

### What to include in your report

*   A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
*   Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
*   Any relevant code snippets or configuration files.

### Response Timeline

*   We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours.
*   We will provide an estimated timeline for the fix within 5 business days.
*   We will notify you when the fix has been released.

### Bounty Program

We currently do not have an official bug bounty program, but we may offer rewards for critical vulnerabilities at our discretion.
